About Me

Assistant Professor and Former Game Developer Based in India

I am an Assistant Professor and Unity Game Developer with over 5 years of industry experience in designing immersive, high-performance gaming experiences. My career blends academic excellence with real-world development, enabling me to bridge the gap between theory and practical application for my students.
With strong expertise in Unity, game design, scripting, and performance optimization, I develop cross-platform games focused on engaging gameplay and seamless user experiences. I am proficient in C#, C, and C++, and continuously stay updated with emerging technologies in interactive media.
As an educator, I am passionate about hands-on learning and mentoring students to build industry-ready skills through creativity, innovation, and critical thinking. Whether in the classroom or development studio, my goal is to create impactful experiences that inspire growth and excellence.
